Registration

Registration Fee

  • The registration fee DOES NOT cover the traveling fee and hotel fee.
  • One registration per presentation. If multiple papers are accepted for presentation, each paper requires a separate registration fee.
  • Multiple submissions of the same author will be accepted; however, one registration is limited to one submission.
  • This is an international conference, we provide only INVOICE, not the FAPIAO.
  • Refund policy: All paid registration fee is non refundable.
  • Student delegates include only enrolled Undergraduate, Master and Ph.D., who are required to provide Student ID Cards for registration.

Please fill in your registration information in the file "Registration.docx", then send your completed "Registration" form, Remittance Voucher (specify: CMMM2024+Name+MS-XX), and Copy of Student ID Cards to the mailbox "CMMM2024@163.com" with the email subject as "Registration: CMMM2024+Name+MS-XX".

Payment Method

Payment Guide

  1. Change the quantity of registration.
  2. Add Additional Banquet Ticket, you can add up to one here, please contact us if you need to add more than one.
  3. Make sure to enter a valid email address to receive a receipt.
  4. Enter the Abstract Acceptance No. you received after submitting your abstract.
  5. Credit cards are recommended. When using a UnionPay card, it has been tested that Class I accounts can make the payment (while Class II accounts fails) and the CVC can be filled with 000.
  6. After successful payment, you will receive an email which can be used as a Remittance Voucher.
